128 When My Teacher Is Somewhere Else My name is Andrea. My teacher’s name is Mrs. Smith. Most school days, Mrs. Smith teaches the class. Sometimes, she has to be somewhere else. Teachers get sick. Mrs. Smith may be sick. She may need to stay home. Teachers go to teacher workshops. Mrs. Smith may go to a teacher workshop. Many teachers have children, and their children get sick. Mrs. Smith has triplets. One of her triplets may be sick, so she needs to stay home. We have a substitute teacher when Mrs. Smith needs to be somewhere else for the day. This is okay.
