14 Chairs 
A chair is a piece of furniture. 
Furniture makes a room ready to use. 
Tables, beds, dressers, chests, and things like that are furniture. 
People sit on chairs to eat, rest, read, relax, or work. People use chairs indoors and outdoors. 
A chair is a useful piece of furniture.
