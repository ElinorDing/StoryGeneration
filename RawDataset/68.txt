66 How to Open a Gift Sometimes people give me gifts. I am learning what to think, say, and do when I get a gift. It takes time and sometimes money to find and wrap a gift. For this reason, it’s thoughtful to say “thank you” right when I get a gift (even before I open it). This means, Thank you for thinking of me, and for getting this gift ready to give to me. It may be okay to open a gift right away. Or, it may be important to wait until later to open a gift. After I open a gift, it’s important to say “thank you.” I am learning what to think, say, and do when I get a gift.
