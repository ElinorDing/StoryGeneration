148 Telling My Teacher about a Problem Teachers and students talk about many things. They often talk about good news. They can also solve problems together.	 Sometimes, a student may have a prob- lem, or feel frustrated or angry. Telling the teacher can help. That way, the teacher will know there is a problem. Teachers want to help. They have a lot of ideas. Teachers can help to solve problems. If I have a problem at school, telling the teacher may help. If I feel frustrated or angry, telling the teacher may help, too. My teacher has a lot of ideas. She can help to solve problems.
