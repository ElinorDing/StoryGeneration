46 Can Mistakes Happen on a Good Day? Can mistakes happen on a good day? I guess it’s possibly true. With so many mistakes that people can make, It’s likely we’ll each make a few. So, do mistakes happen every day? If people wake up, then yes. But what if they all were to stay in their beds? Well, that’s a mistake, I guess. Can mistakes happen on a good day? It seems that maybe they do.
