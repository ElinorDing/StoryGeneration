64 Why Do People Wrap Gifts? Many people wrap gifts before they give them away. Why do they do this? Wrapping hides a gift, and helps to keep it a secret. Later, when the gift is opened, it’s a surprise. Many people think that nice surprises are fun. A wrapped gift is pretty. Sometimes, there’s colorful paper, a bow, or a card. Many people think that wrapping a gift is a beautiful way to hide a surprise. Wrapped gifts are a part of celebrating special times and feelings.
