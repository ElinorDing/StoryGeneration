74Two-Person HugsSometimes two people hug one another. It’s atwo-person hug when both people hug at the sametime.A two-person hug often means hello or goodbye.Sometimes, people use two-person hugs to sharetheir feelings. These hugs may mean, I love you, Weare both happy, or We are both sad.When two people hug each other at the same time,they often:• Stand close, facing each other• Wrap one or two arms around the other person• Squeeze a little but not a lot• End the hug when either person begins to relax their armsSometimes two people hug each other at the same time.
